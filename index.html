CloudEvents: Live

A real-time debugger and event-sink for CloudEvents. It gives you an endpoint ready to receive webhooks from your application when you're debugging. When you get a new event it shows up instantly in your debugger. Want to host it yourself? - the code including Helm-charts is on Github.
